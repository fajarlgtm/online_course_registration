# Task C – Corrected Requirements

1. The system shall allow only active enrolled students to register for courses.

2. The system shall allow course registration until the official deadline. Late registration shall be permitted for 3 calendar days after the deadline with a $50 late fee.

3. The system shall display real-time available seats for each course.

4. The system shall prevent registration when seats are full and automatically place students on a waitlist.

5. The system shall automatically enroll waitlisted students in the order they joined when a seat becomes available.

6. The system shall prevent registration for overlapping courses unless one course is asynchronous online.

7. The system shall limit students to 18 credit hours per semester. Students may register up to 24 credit hours only after advisor approval recorded in the system.

8. The system shall send a confirmation email within 1 minute of successful registration.

9. The system shall prevent students from registering for courses without meeting prerequisites unless instructor approval is digitally recorded.

10. The system shall allow course drops until the end of Week 6. A $100 fee shall be charged for drops after Week 2.

11. The system shall complete registration transactions within 3 seconds under normal system load.
